- **涉及文件**：`src-tauri/src/commands/ai_config.rs`、`src-tauri/src/ai.rs`、`src-tauri/src/commands/jobs.rs`、`src/app/api/ai-config.ts`、`src/app/pages/AISettings.tsx`
